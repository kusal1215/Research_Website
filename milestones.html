Milestones

Our main milestones in the process of developing the research product.
from Mind Guardian - Happier Life.

  • PROJECT CHARTER

    The primary objective of a project charter is to finalize a suitable topic

    Date of evaluation: 12th February 2021
    Marks: 0%.

  • PROJECT PROPOSAL

    The primary objective of a project proposal is to coordinate time and resources for completing the project with the highest possible standards

    Date of evaluation: 08th - 10th March, 2021
    Marks: 6%.

  • PROGRESS PRESENTATION I

    The evaluation is based on a prototype product that was 50% complete. The partially completed project is reviewed, and suggestions for improvement are provided by the evaluation panel.

    Date of evaluation: 05th - 07th July, 2021
    Marks: 15%.

  • PROGRESS PRESENTATION II

    The evaluation is based on a prototype product that was 90% complete. The project is reviewed, and suggestions for improvement are provided by the evaluation panel. The project is assessed to identify whether the project satisfies the aimed objective.

    Date of evaluation: 18th October, 2021
    Marks: 12%.

  • FINAL ASSESSMENT

    The evaluation is based on the completed project to assess the project achieves the mentioned specifications in the project proposal.

    Date of evaluation: 22nd - 24th November, 2021
    Marks: 10%.

  • FINAL VIVA

    Oral evaluation is based on the completed project.

    Date of evaluation: 22nd - 24th November, 2021
    Marks: 10%.
